Cold Call Script
Your goal is ONE thing: book a meeting. Keep it under 60 seconds.
OPENING
"Hi, is this [Name]? Great — my name is [Your Name] from Costa Blanca Investments. I'll be quick — we've just sold a property in your area and wanted to reach out personally. Have you ever thought about what your property could be worth today?"
IF INTERESTED
"Fantastic. I'd love to pop by for 20 minutes — free, no obligation. Would [DAY] or [DAY] work for you?"
IF NOT INTERESTED
"No problem! Do you know anyone in the area thinking of selling? I'd love to help them."
IF THEY HAVE AN AGENT
"That's great — are you happy with the results so far? If anything changes, we're always here."
💡 Warm, calm, confident. The appointment is the win — not the sale.
🚪
Door Knocking Script
Under 30 seconds. You're delivering value, not selling.
OPENING
"Hi! Sorry to disturb you — my name is [Name] from Costa Blanca Investments. We just sold a property on [Street] and wanted to let neighbours know personally — in case you've wondered what your home might be worth today."
IF INTERESTED
"We're seeing really strong prices right now. I'd love to give you a free valuation — takes about 20 minutes. Would that be useful?"
IF NOT SELLING
"Completely understand! Do you know anyone on the street who might be thinking about it?" [Leave card]
💡 Step back when the door opens. Smile. Every conversation is practice.

Viewing Scripts
Help them fall in love — then ask for the decision.
WELCOME
"Welcome! Take your time — no rush. Explore first, I'm here if you have questions."
EMOTIONAL QUESTIONS
"Could you see yourself having breakfast on this terrace?" / "What do you think of the views?" / "How does this compare to the others you've seen?"
CLOSING
"What did you think overall? Is this something you'd want to move forward on?" [WAIT IN SILENCE]
IF MAYBE
"What would need to be different for this to be a clear yes?" [This is the real objection]
💡 After the closing question — say NOTHING. Silence wins every time.

3 DAYS LATER

BREAK-UP MESSAGE

💡 The break-up message gets 30–40% reply rate. It works.

Objection Handling
Every objection is a question in disguise.
"PRICE IS TOO HIGH"
"What price would make you want to move forward today?" [Get their number] "Let me take that to the seller."
"I NEED TO THINK"
"What specifically? The price, location, or condition? Let's talk it out right now."
"NEED TO SPEAK TO PARTNER"
"Can we set up a call with both of you this week so I can answer everything together?"
"WANT TO SEE MORE"
"What specifically are you still looking for? That way the next one will be even closer to perfect."
💡 Agree first, then ask a question. Arguments end deals. Questions keep them alive.

Closing Scripts
Closing is just asking for a decision. Confident agents ask. Scared agents wait.
DIRECT CLOSE
"Are you ready to put an offer in? I can prepare the paperwork right now." [Stop talking. Wait.]
SUMMARY CLOSE
"You love the location, the size works, the price fits. The only thing left is to make it official. Shall we?"
URGENCY CLOSE
"There's another party seriously interested. If this is the one, let's secure it now."
AFTER THEY SAY YES
"Fantastic! Let's get the documents sorted right now." [Do NOT celebrate. Move fast. Paperwork first.]
💡 After every closing question — SILENCE. Count to 10. Never fill the silence.

Door Knocking SOP
The fastest way to generate listings in your farm area. Do this consistently and you will get results within 30 days.
1
Prepare your farm area
Choose 200–300 homes in one specific neighbourhood — Altea Hills, El Albir, Calpe town. Print a simple one-page flyer: recent sale price in the area, your name, CBI logo, phone number. This is your reason to knock.
2
Best times to knock
Weekdays 10:00–13:00 and 17:00–19:30. Avoid Mondays and Fridays. Saturday mornings (10:00–12:00) are gold for expat villa owners who are home.
3
The opening 20 seconds
Step back when the door opens. Smile. Speak first.
"Hi! Sorry to disturb you — my name is [Name] from Costa Blanca Investments. We just sold a villa on [nearby street] and I wanted to personally let the neighbours know — in case you've been curious what your home might be worth right now."
4
If they show interest
"We're seeing very strong prices right now — especially for villas with sea views or private pools. I'd love to pop by for 20 minutes and give you a free no-obligation valuation. Would that be useful? Would [DAY] or [DAY] work for you?"
💡 Stop talking after asking. The silence is your friend.
5
If they're not selling
"Completely understand! Do you know anyone on this street who might be thinking about it? I'd love to help them."
Leave your card. Note the address. Come back in 3 months. Consistency builds trust.
6
Track everything
Log every door in your Pipeline tab: address, response, follow-up date. Aim for 30 doors per session. You need 3–5 sessions per street before you're known there.
✅ Target: 1 valuation appointment per 20–30 doors. That's completely normal — don't stop.

Viewing SOP
A viewing is not a tour. It's a structured process to help buyers fall in love and make a decision. Follow these steps every single time.
1
Before the viewing (30 mins prior)
Arrive early. Open shutters, turn on lights, open terrace doors. The property should feel alive and welcoming. Check the pool is clean and the garden looks tidy. If there's a sea view — make sure nothing is blocking it.
2
The welcome — set the tone
"Welcome! Please take your time — no rush at all. Feel free to explore first, and I'll be here whenever you have questions."
Do NOT lead them room by room like a tour guide. Let them wander. People buy with emotion — give them space to feel it.
3
Emotional anchor questions
Ask these naturally as you move through the property:
"Could you see yourself having breakfast on this terrace?"
"What do you think of the views from up here?"
"How does this feel compared to the others you've seen?"
"Which room do you think you'd use most?"
4
The closing question
When you feel the energy shift (slower movements, longer looks, quiet conversation between them) — ask:
"What did you think overall? Is this something you'd want to move forward on?"
💡 After this question — say NOTHING. Count to 10 in your head. Whoever speaks first loses. Wait.
5
If they say "Maybe" or "We need to think"
"What specifically would need to be different for this to be a clear yes?"
This gets you the real objection. Price, location, size, condition — then you can address it directly.
6
Same day follow-up (within 2 hours)
"Hi [Name]! Really enjoyed showing you the property today. Any questions coming up? Happy to answer anything."
🚫 Never speak badly about another property they've seen. Never rush them. Never answer your phone during a viewing.
⚡
New Lead SOP
Speed is everything. The agent who responds first wins. Your goal in the first 5 minutes is to qualify, connect, and book an appointment.
1
Respond within 5 minutes
Studies show response time within 5 minutes = 9x higher conversion. Every minute after that your chances drop. Drop everything and call or WhatsApp immediately.
2
First WhatsApp message
"Hi [Name]! 👋 This is [Your Name] from Costa Blanca Investments. I saw you enquired about [property/area]. Are you looking to buy in the next 3–6 months?"
3
Qualify with 4 questions
When they respond, find out:
1. "What area are you most interested in?"
2. "What's your budget range?"
3. "Are you buying cash or with a mortgage?"
4. "When are you planning to visit the Costa Blanca?"
4
Book the appointment
"Perfect — I have 3 properties in [area] that match exactly what you're looking for. When you're here, I'd love to show you around. Would [DATE] work for you or would [DATE] be better?"
💡 Always give two options — never ask an open question. Two options forces a decision.
5
Add to Pipeline immediately
Log the lead in your Pipeline tab within 10 minutes: name, source, budget, area, follow-up date. A lead not logged is a lead you'll forget.
✅ First goal: Book a call or viewing — not a sale. The appointment is the win.
🔄
Follow-Up SOP
80% of sales happen after the 5th contact. Most agents stop after 1. This SOP is why top agents consistently out-earn everyone else.
1
Same day — after viewing
"Hi [Name]! Really enjoyed showing you around today. Any questions popping up? Happy to answer anything 😊"
2
Day 3 — new property match
"Hi [Name]! A new villa just came onto the market in [area] — [2 line description], €[price]. I immediately thought of you. Want me to arrange a viewing?"
3
Day 7 — market update
"Hi [Name]! Quick update — we've had 3 new enquiries this week on properties in [area]. The market is moving fast right now. Still looking? I have something that might be perfect."
4
Day 14 — value add
"Hi [Name] — I put together a short overview of average prices in [area] for the past 3 months. Would you like me to send it over? Might be useful for your search."
5
Day 30 — the break-up message
"Hi [Name] — I don't want to keep bothering you if the timing isn't right. I'll leave it here for now, but I'm always one message away if you need anything. Best of luck! 👋"
💡 This message gets a 30–40% response rate. People feel guilty ignoring it. It works every time.
🚫 Never follow up more than once per week unless they've engaged. Never send the same message twice.

Get a Listing SOP
Listings are the inventory of your business. Without listings, everything else stops. This is the process to get sellers to sign with CBI.
1
Book the valuation appointment
Use the door-knocking, cold-calling, or referral process to get in front of a seller. The only goal of the first call is to book a face-to-face valuation appointment — not to list the property.
2
Before the appointment — prepare
Research the property: land registry, comparable sales in the area, how long similar properties have been on the market. Use the CBI Valuation tool to generate a professional PDF report to bring with you.
3
The valuation meeting — listen first
"Before I share my thoughts, I'd love to understand your situation. What's your reason for considering a move? And if you did sell — where would you go next?"
The answers to these two questions tell you everything you need to know about their motivation and timeline.
4
Present your price — confidently
Show 3 comparable sales. Be honest. Do not inflate the price to win the listing — you will lose the client 90 days later when the property doesn't sell.
"Based on what's sold in the last 6 months and what's active right now, the right price to attract serious buyers is €[X]. This is what gets you the best result in the shortest time."
5
Handle the price objection
"I completely understand you feel it's worth more — and it may well be. But what we've seen consistently is that overpriced properties sit on the market for 6–12 months and end up selling for less than if they'd been priced correctly from day one. My job is to get you the best outcome, not just the listing."
6
Ask for the listing
"I'd love to put CBI's full network behind this property and get it sold. Shall we go ahead and get the paperwork signed today?"
💡 Then stop talking. Wait for the answer.
✅ Leave every valuation with either a signed contract or a firm follow-up date. Never leave without one of these two.
🤝
Close a Deal SOP
Closing is not a trick. It's asking for a decision at the right moment. Confident agents ask early and often. Scared agents wait and lose.
1
Read the signals
Buying signals: they start measuring rooms, they ask about neighbours, they talk about where their furniture would go, they ask about schools, they go quiet and thoughtful. When you see these — it's time to close.
2
The direct close
"Are you ready to put an offer in? I can get the paperwork started right now."
💡 Stop talking. Wait. Do NOT fill the silence.
3
The summary close
"You love the location, the size works for your family, the price is within your budget, and the views are exactly what you were looking for. The only thing left to do is make it official. Shall we?"
4
The urgency close
"I have to be honest with you — there's another couple who viewed this last week and they're coming back tomorrow. If this is the one for you, now is the time to move. I don't want you to miss it."
5
After they say yes
"Fantastic! Let's get the reservation sorted right now while we're together."
Do NOT celebrate loudly. Do NOT hug them or make them feel sold to. Stay calm, stay professional, start the paperwork immediately. Movement = commitment.
6
Notify the office
WhatsApp the secretary immediately: property ref, buyer name, reservation amount, expected closing date. Log the deal in the Revenue Growth chart.
🚫 Never negotiate against yourself. Never reduce your fee without getting something in return. Never let a deal die over 2–3% if both parties are close.

Daily Routine SOP
Top agents are not more talented. They are more consistent. This is the daily structure that separates the top 5% from everyone else.
1
07:00 — Morning ritual (30 mins)
No phone for the first 30 minutes. Exercise, or at minimum a 15-minute walk. Review your goals for the day. Read or listen to something that sharpens your mindset — not the news.
2
08:00 — Power hour: calls and follow-ups
This is the most important hour of your day. Make your calls before your brain gets distracted by emails, messages, and admin. Target 20 calls per day minimum. Log every single one in KPI.
Rule: No meetings, no viewings, no admin before your calls are done.
3
10:00 — Pipeline review
Check your Pipeline. Who needs a follow-up today? Who viewed last week and went quiet? Who has a viewing booked that needs confirmation? Update every lead's status.
4
11:00–17:00 — Appointments, viewings, valuations
This is your prime client time. Use the CBI Valuation tool to prepare for any listing presentations. Arrive at viewings 30 minutes early. Always have a property brochure ready.
5
17:00 — Admin and prospecting round 2
Update listings, send follow-up messages, respond to enquiries, check email. A second round of WhatsApp follow-ups from 17:00–18:30 works well — people are home from work.
6
End of day — log and plan
Log your daily activity in the KPI tab before you close the laptop. Write down tomorrow's top 3 priorities. A day planned the night before is a day that starts with momentum.
✅ Non-negotiables: 20 calls · 1 prospecting activity · Pipeline updated · Activity logged. Miss these and you fall behind. Do these and you build a business.

Social Media SOP
Social media is the new door knocking. Done right, it brings leads to you. Done wrong, it wastes hours with no return. Follow this system.
1
Your content mix — 3 types
40% Property content — listings, just sold, just listed, price reductions
40% Market knowledge — area guides, price trends, neighbourhood info, local tips
20% Personal brand — you at a viewing, behind the scenes, your wins, your team
2
Post frequency
Instagram: 4–5x per week (Reels perform best). Facebook: 3x per week. WhatsApp Status: daily — quick updates, just listed, just sold. Consistency over quality. Done is better than perfect.
3
Best performing content for Costa Blanca
— "Just sold in [area] for €X" with a photo of the view
— Short walking video tour of a listing (30–60 seconds)
— "Price drop" announcements — these get massive reach
— "What €500k buys you in Altea vs Javea" comparison posts
— Sunrise/sunset property shots — always perform well
4
The caption formula
Hook (line 1) → Value (2–4 lines) → Call to action (final line)

Example:
"This villa in Altea Hills just sold in 3 weeks. 🏡
4 beds, private pool, sea views — €895,000.
The market is moving fast right now.
DM me if you're thinking of selling — I'd love to help."
5
Engage — don't just post
Spend 15 minutes per day engaging with your followers' content. Reply to every comment within 1 hour. DM everyone who likes a property post — they're warm leads. The algorithm rewards activity, not just posts.
6
Convert social media to real conversations
"Hi [Name]! I noticed you liked our post about the Altea villa — are you actively looking in that area? I have a couple of off-market properties that might interest you."
💡 Every like, comment, or save is a warm lead. Chase them all.
🚫 Never argue, complain, or post anything negative on social media. You are a brand. Every post is a first impression for someone who doesn't know you yet.

VI · Voice & Tone

How It Speaks

“

Direct. Clean. Sharp. Written with the authority of a brokerage owner, not the eagerness of a junior agent.

Do · The Rules

  • Refer to Bruno Felipe by name in third-person marketing.
  • Write for an international, multilingual audience.
  • Lead with expertise and local knowledge.
  • Keep sentences tight. Say it once, say it well.
  • Default sign-off: Kind Regards · Met vriendelijke groet · Saludos cordiales.

Don't · Forbidden

  • Never use: nestled · hidden gem · boasting.
  • Never use: stunning · dream home · paradise.
  • No generic Mediterranean clichés.
  • No first-person "we" in listings or bios.
  • No fluff, no filler, no hype without substance.
